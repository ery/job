主要是Ruby开发，Java/PHP开发的愿意转的热烈欢迎。

年薪20-35万+期权+补贴.联系邮箱：talent@isofttalent.com

Job Description

An engineer in application development team will

  • Design, build and operate our flagship products including advertising system and billing system.
  • Work independently or with mentor’s help to make day-to-day tech decision to grow our global business.

Qualification

  • Capabilities of logical and rational thinking.
  • Strongly self-motivated.
  • Hands-on web system development and design experience. Solid knowledge in the field (e.g. http, LAMP, JAVA, cutting-edge technologies), Ruby on Rails is preferred.
  • Good English skills on listening, speaking, reading and writing.
  • Master’s degree in Computer Science or related field is a plus.

不限您现在用什么开发语言，只要您熟悉算法/计算机基础。

年薪25万-45万/年

有意者请发简历至 talent@isofttalent.com

工作内容：

  • 负责网站前端和后台的开发；
  • 数据分析整合，算法设计等工程挑战等着您（不一定涉及非常复杂的算法，也不是big data等流行方向）。

应聘资格：

  • 名校毕业；或者非名校毕业但是有竞赛背景，或者喜欢混迹topcoder/Github等；
  • a geek in computer science and excellent in algorithm and data structure;
  • Be versed in Ruby, Python, JavaScript, C++, Java, C# or other programming language;
  • A plus: web, website or Adobe Flash Player developing experience.
