If you have at 1 year experience in Ruby on Rails, with this knowledge, please contact me (with your full resume in English) - amy@pagerise.com - we sincerely regret to inform that only shortlisted candidates will be notified.

Junior Developer, Ruby-on-Rails

We are hiring an aspiring Junior Ruby developer. But read these before you apply:

Do you excel in a fast-paced, demanding environment?
Do building great things and working in a cutting edge technology environment excite you?
Do you have the self-motivation and organized mindset to get things done?
Can you manage your time and tasks and work effectively?
Do you possess the passion to write great code and interest in building highly successful and scalable service to meet customer demands?

Proceed only if you say YES to all questions, otherwise application is meaningless.

KEY FUNCTIONS & WEB DEVELOPMENT REQUIREMENT

Work directly with company founders and development team to produce and maintain a number of web applications
Work with Ruby based platform, in conjunction with MySQL or PostgreSQL
Work with Ajax and JavaScript code to deliver seamless user interactions (we also use jQuery framework)
Must be comfortable with MVC design pattern
Demonstrate experience in reading and modifying the code written by other developers
Demonstrate experience in test driven development, or at a minimum, maintain a suite of developer unit tests
Experience in delivering scalable application (both the frontend load balancing and backend expansion)
Proficient in using source control tools (like Git)
Have e-commerce experience, such as secure financial transaction
Passionate for software development and Object Oriented design
Familiarity with social networking sites and best practices for integrating with them Proficient in Linux system (predominantly ubuntu)

ATTITUDES AND VALUES

Exercise your design creativities to get things done, driven by results
Be a self-motivator (independent) who can take direction, and more importantly, initiator who comes with plausible solutions for problems
Possess communication and interpersonal skills that encourage growth and foster team development
Be flexible and can handle multiple tasks

Apply only if you have Ruby on Rails specific experience:

About 1 + years of Working knowledge with Ruby on Rails
Experience in Ruby gems and apply them to our products (essentially, build your code on top of work contributed by open community / plugins / gems)

A STRONG ADVANTAGE FOR APPLICANTS, who possess one or all of the followings:

SEO experience
Working knowledge of building iPhone / Android apps using cross platform mobile framework
Working knowledge of building RESTful web services to enable unified digital experiences across mobile, social and web platforms
Speak and write Japanese

In case you want to know why our staff choose and stay with ONENRG? Here are some reasons why:

Work and become friends with a positive team of passionate people
Enjoy an exciting, fast-paced, demanding environment
Being a significant part of building something great
Play pool when a break is needed
Chill with a breezer after work
Easily accessible to our new office @ 71 Ayer Rajah Crescent (via One-North MRT station)

Your application needs to reflect understanding of the requirements above – how to do it? Make sure your work experience truly reflects what we want and you tell us why you want to join us. Otherwise it shall not be entertained.

Salary and other conditions:

S$2400 - S$2800 negotiable
Relevant work pass will be applied for selected candidates
