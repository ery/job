[武汉] 招聘Ruby on Rails开发工程师

招聘 • bydmm • 于发布 • 最后由 actberw 于回复 • 1184次阅读

工作地点:武汉

Ruby on Rails开发工程师

职位描述:
我们正在寻找经验丰富，充满激情的Ruby on Rails开发人员加入我们在中国的开发团队。这支团队将提供程序开发和技术支持，包括设计和开发一个高度可扩展的网站和增加许多新的令人兴奋的功能。

任职资格:
- 你是一个真正的开发工程师，而不仅仅只是一个系统管理员。
- 你在Ruby on Rails 单元测试和自动化集成方面有丰富经验, 在网络基础架构方面有丰富开发经验者优先。
- 你在JavaScript/HTML/CSS和跨浏览器的兼容性问题方面有丰富的开发经验。
- 你在Linux, AWS (S3/EC2) 和基于云计算技术方面有相关的工作经验
- 你在代码方面追求干净和清洁，追求完美。
- 你有两年以上的实际工作经验- 在RoR编程与开发方面有经验者优先

职位要求:
- 具有出色的书面和口头沟通能力。（良好的英语理解能力是必备的。）
- 具有积极的态度，努力工作和按时完成任务的愿望，有自觉独立完成工作的能力
- 追求细节，自我激励，有组织，独立，采取所有权分配工作。
- 诚实，热衷学习新的东西

福利待遇:
- 具有竞争力的工资
- 灵活的工作时间
- 健康福利
- 节假日薪酬
- 可供选择的计算机系统
- 奖励津贴 (根据公司及个人表现)
o 节假日礼物
o 节假日礼物
o 年终奖金
o 住房津贴
o 公费旅行-旧金山之旅
o 其他…(我们将暂时将其作为惊喜)

有意者请发送简历至 info[AT]evebit.com

Ruby on Rails Developer

Job Description:
We are looking for experienced, passionate Ruby on Rails developers to become a part of our development team in China. This team will be providing programming & technical support including designing and developing a highly scalable website and many new exciting features.

Only apply if:
- You are a true engineer, not just a system admin.
- You are experienced in Ruby on Rails, unit testing, and automated integration
- You are experienced with online media delivery infrastructure preferred
- You are experienced in JavaScript/HTML/CSS and cross-browser compatibility issues
- You have working knowledge of Linux, AWS (S3/EC2) and cloud-based technology
- You write clean codes and are never satisfied with anything less than perfect.
- You have 2+ years real work experience - in a RoR programming/developing role preferred

What we are looking for::
- Excellent written and oral communication skills. (Good understanding of English is a must.)
- You have a positive attitude, the desire to work hard and meet deadlines, and the ability to work independently with little supervision.
- Extremely detail oriented, self-motivated, organized, self-sufficient, and take ownership of work assigned.
- Honest and always hungry to learn new things

Compensation:
- Competitive salary
- Flexible work hours
- Health benefits
- Paid time-off and holiday pay
- A computer system of your choice
- Bonus perks (based on company and individual’s performance)
o Holiday gifts
o Year-end bonus
o Housing assistance
o Fully covered trip to visit San Francisco
o More... (we’ll keep those as surprises)

Please email your resume to info[AT]evebit.com
