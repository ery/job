Position summary

The Adobe Digital Media BJ team is looking for a Sr. Software Engineer to help build the next generation of Creative Extensibility Ecosystem. We're looking for a strong Sr. Engineer which will ensure the integrity and success of a critical piece of the Adobe Creative Extensibility Ecosystem. If you enjoy working with advanced technologies, have a proven track record of strong performance, aren't intimidated by aggressive goals, and have a proven commitment to delivering great software, this may be the role for you.

Responsibilities

  • Provide a leading role in designing, enhancing, and maintaining Adobe Creative Extensibility Ecosystem solutions using cutting-edge technologies.
  • Coordinate efforts with the global engineering teams to ensure the needs of Adobe partners and developers are met and exceeded.
  • Follow agile software development methodologies to ensure all milestones and commitments are achieved.
  • Solve complex business and technical problems involving Adobe desktop applications’ extensibility, online marketplace for creative extensions etc.
  • Work with the Quality Engineering team to design and automate tests for functional and API testing to ensure complete test coverage for all areas of the product with a meticulous attention to detail.
  • Address software bugs, write automated tests, and quickly address any issues introduced in the development environment.

Requirements

  • 6+ years Software Engineering experience or equivalent industry experience
  • Master or Bachelor degree in computer science or a related technical field.
  • Experience in Internet / web-based software development or testing
  • Expert in design patterns, distributed systems and caching services
  • Extensive experience with Web Services (SOAP and REST)
  • Strong proficiency in mid-tier / server-side web application development using with ROR (Ruby on Rails), Java and large databases
  • Strong proficiency in front technology with JavaScript, AJAX, CSS, etc.
  • Strong experience with designing a highly scalable automation infrastructure
  • Strong experience with web application frameworks
  • Strong understanding of how to take requirements from multiple sources and create a new feature.
  • Ability to analyze, isolate, and document complex technical issues
  • Ability to achieve results with little supervision or direction
  • Ability to avoid distractions and deliver against deadlines
  • Must have a positive attitude, be self-motivated, detail-oriented, and quick learner
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills, be proactive about working with others and be able to articulate ideas clearly
  • Excellent time management, communication, decision making, presentation, human relations, and organization skills
  • Willing to work in a highly competitive environment
