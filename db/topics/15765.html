We're looking for hard-working, passion, and talented developers who have foucsed developing Rails applications. You will join the Agile team, will management company along with all workmates and continuous high growth.

Ruby developer requirements（Salary：6k-12k）
1. knowledgeable about both front-end and the back-end development, well-designed.
2. well-communication to enjoy working with clients to tailor solutions for their required.
3. nice have skills and tools: Ruby, Rails, Rspec, HTML, CSS, SASS, LESS, jquery, Backbone.js,MySQL, Postgres, Heroku, git, caching, API development, browser compatibility, database tuning and so on(more or less)
4. both in senior or junior.
5. excellent experience with Agile Thinking

ruby实习生 职位要求：
1, 有良好的计算机基础知识;

4, 热爱编程. 希望成为一个Geeker;
5, 有良好的沟通能力;
6, 英语至少能读写.
工作地点：武汉

如果你现在还是在校学生，但对ruby有兴趣，并且英文读写熟练，我们愿意为你提供实习机会，实习期后可以入职公司
