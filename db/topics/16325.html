Location: Haidian District, Beijing (北京海淀区上地软件园)

Company: 北京依诺维尔科技有限公司 （50-99人）

Compensation: Competitive (DOE) and all benefits

About Innover:

Innover is dedicated to providing customers the innovative talent, services, and tools to create world class software solutions. We strive to exceed customer expectations and deliver value through distinctive expertise, while cultivating a work environment that fosters teamwork, pride, accomplishment, growth, balance, and enjoyment for our employees.
For over 7 years, Innover has been providing leading U.S. companies with experienced software consultants and specialized outsourced project development enabling them to create world class solutions. Our commitment to providing a challenging environment focused on teamwork and growth has been paramount to building and retaining an experienced team of IT professionals. Our team boasts exceptional knowledge of current technologies and the IT industry, and has extensive relationships with companies and workers throughout the world.

About the Position:

Our client is in need of an experienced lead/senior RoR developer to lead a growing team of developers to work on the development of interactive marketing solutions. This is an exciting opportunity to join a dynamic team using some of the most cutting edge open-source technologies.
This opportunity will give you the chance to work with a distinguished portfolio of clients and collaborate with skilled team members to develop new creative ideas. If you are interested in an exciting development opportunity that offers the chance to work with some of the newest open source technology than this is the position for you.

Requirements:

  • 2+ years of experience working on a production Rails app required
  • 5+ years of overall programming experience preferred
  • Startup mentality
  • JavaScript experience (JQuery)
  • HTML/CSS
  • Good working knowledge of relational databases
  • Caching techniques at the object and page level
  • Spoken and Written English required
  • Short-term travel to San Francisco, US office may be required from time to time

Contact:
